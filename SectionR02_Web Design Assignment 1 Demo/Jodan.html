Jodan was a character that was created before Quarantine but since this month was her canonical birthday,
she ended up getting a lot of attention from me in terms of development. For those who don't know (because why would you, honestly),
Jodan is a kitsune who was sealed away 1000 years ago for a crime she didn't commit. Having gone mad from the forced isolation, (how topical..)
she tends to steal the souls of lost travelers who fail her Tests of Heart. If you lack proper manners, you can kiss your soul goodbye.


"How unfortunate."
